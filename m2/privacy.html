6. Security

How we protect your data

We take the security of your data seriously. Our measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption across all platform pages and API endpoints
  • Bcrypt password hashing — your password is never stored in plain text
  • OTP-based verification for password resets and sensitive account actions
  • Session tokens with expiry and secure HTTP-only cookies
  • Regular security audits and bot detection on all forms
  • Admin access controls with audit logs

Despite our best efforts, no system is 100% secure. If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact support immediately and change your password.

7. Data Retention

How long we keep your information

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and for a period thereafter as required by law:

  • Transaction records: retained for 5 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations
  • Communications: retained for 12 months from the date of the communication
  • Log data: retained for 90 days then automatically deleted
